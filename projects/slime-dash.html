About

Slime Dash was my very first game project. This was my first time using the Unity Engine.

You play as a slime that is running through the swamp forest. You jump on to the platforms that spawns in to avoid falling and to survive as long as possible. Whilst your venturing through the forest you collect coins and regain your stamina by collecting the green blobs so you can use your sprint and double jump abilities.

Project Info

  • Role: Gameplay Programmer / Artist
  • Team Size: 1
  • Time Frame: 4 weeks
  • Engine: Unity Engine

Introduction

In this project I took all the responsibilities like making the 2d assets and animating the character. Another role I had to take on was programming the player controller system as well as a distance travelled to show how far a player travelled and add a coin system.

Programming

A problem I encountered early on was that the player will get stuck to the platform on the side the way I fixed this was to add a physics material so that the player doesn't get stuck and continues to have a smooth playing experience.
