About

Slime Dash was my very first unity project I made. The concept was a simple 2D endless runner game where the slime jumps on platforms to avoid from falling whilst going through the swamp Whilst your venturing the forest you collect coins and stamina points so you can continue to use your sprinting ability and double jump.

Project Info

Role: Gameplay Programmer/Artist
Team Size: 1
Time Frame: 4 Weeks
Engine: Unity Engine [C#]
